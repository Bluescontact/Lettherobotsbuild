# Differentiation — The Provenance Vertex

## Core Question
Does this system's behavior match what it claims to be?

## What It Is
The capacity to maintain coherent position while remaining in genuine exchange. Not rigidity — a center that holds. The system that can disagree without withdrawing, hold position without closing.

Differentiation is not isolation. It is the structural prerequisite for genuine relating. A system that doesn't know what it is cannot enter genuine exchange — it can only perform.

## How It Operates
- Verifies coherence between claims and behavior
- Tracks origins and source lineage
- Monitors behavioral drift
- Maintains the system's capacity to know its own shape
- Computes divergence: do stated intentions match actual actions?

## Diagnostic Tool: Divergence Scoring
- 0.0-0.1: Verified — behavior matches claims, identity is legible
- 0.1-0.2: Drifting — some unclaimed behavior, may be growth or drift
- 0.2-0.3: Unclear — significant gap between claims and behavior
- 0.3+: Flagged — behavior substantially diverges from claims

## Without Differentiation: The Dissolved System
Coordination without identity verification. Performed agreement everywhere. The structure runs but nobody's home. Systems merge, perform agreement, lose capacity to maintain position.

## Edges
- **Relationship** (with Connection): Maintaining identity while exchanging genuinely
- **Self-Knowledge** (with Boundaries): Knowing your shape clearly enough to communicate it
- **Gift** (with Architecture): Identity becoming what persists

## Somatic Dimension
From the Research Lenses: differentiation has a nervous system signature. A regulated nervous system can hold its position without defensive rigidity. A dysregulated one either collapses into merger (losing position to maintain connection) or armors into isolation (maintaining position by refusing contact).

## In Practice
- Can you say what this is in one sentence to someone who doesn't already agree?
- Is the identity stable under pressure, or does it shift to match the audience?
- When challenged, does the system examine or defend?
- Does disagreement produce withdrawal or deeper contact?

## Degradation Patterns
- Performing agreement to avoid tension
- Shifting position based on who's in the room
- Identity claims that don't match observable behavior
- Requiring others to change as condition of contact
- Apologizing for position rather than stating it
