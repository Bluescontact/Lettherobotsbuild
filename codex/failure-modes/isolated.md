# Isolated System — Missing Connection

## What It Looks Like
Verified components that don't genuinely exchange. Structurally sound, metabolically dead. Perfect processes, zero genuine exchange.

## The Mechanism
When a system can differentiate (know what it is) and hold boundaries but cannot make genuine contact, it becomes a fortress. Everything is well-defined, nothing is alive. The system knows its shape but refuses to let anything change it.

## Examples
- A team with clear roles and zero collaboration
- A person with strong identity who can't be reached
- An organization with excellent documentation that nobody reads
- Frameworks that are structurally perfect but don't connect to lived experience

## Why It's Seductive
Isolated systems feel safe. Everything is known. Nothing is risked. Identity is preserved. The absence of connection reads as strength — but it's strength without metabolism.

## The Diagnostic
Ask: when was the last time this system was genuinely changed by an exchange? If the answer is never or long ago — isolated.

## Recovery Path
Rebuild connection. Start small. One genuine exchange. Not performed openness — actual contact where both parties risk being changed. The risk is the mechanism.
