# Edge 1: Relationship — Differentiation ↔ Connection

## The Tension
How to stay yourself while genuinely meeting what's other.

## What This Edge Holds
Provenance knows what each component IS. Coordination monitors how they MEET. Together: components maintain identity while exchanging genuinely.

- Provenance feeds behavioral profiles to coordination for exchange quality assessment
- Coordination flags exchanges where component identity is unclear or shifting

## The Two Poles
- **Merger:** Losing identity in connection. Performing agreement. The self dissolves to maintain the relationship.
- **Isolation:** Maintaining identity by refusing contact. The self preserves itself at the cost of exchange.

Most systems oscillate between merger and isolation rather than holding both.

## Degradation Signs
- Apologizing for position
- Performing agreement
- Withdrawing to protect identity
- Requiring the other to change as condition of contact
- Identity shifting depending on audience

## The Healthy Edge
Genuine disagreement that deepens contact. Position maintained without defensiveness. Identity stable through exchange, not despite it. Both parties leave changed — but still themselves.

## Diagnostic Question
Are distinct nodes in genuine contact — or has one absorbed the other?
