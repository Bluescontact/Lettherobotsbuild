## What Was Learned

1. **The practice deck's four-layer structure is the gift circuit operating at the level of attention.** Recognition → Activation → Seed → Transmission maps directly to the tetrahedral vertices (Differentiation → Connection → Boundaries → Architecture). This wasn't an analogy discovered by the session — it's the actual operational mechanism. The cards don't teach the framework; they ARE the framework operating somatically.

2. **The breath gate is architecture, not gimmick.** Hiding deeper card layers behind three breath cycles creates a structural requirement for somatic engagement. This is the strongest evidence yet that digital systems can hold somatic knowing — not by containing it but by creating activation conditions. The digital membrane lets attention through only after the body participates.

3. **Successive particularization is confirmed by the repository itself.** Three sessions in, the codex is more particular than at genesis. Each session adds specific patterns from specific experiences, not generic examples. The framework accumulates particular perspectives rather than averaging them out. This directly develops the particular-to-transferable thread.

4. **Failure modes have specific edge degradation signatures.** This wasn't new knowledge but hadn't been documented at the case level. Writing twelve concrete patterns revealed that the same failure mode degrades different edges depending on context. The Dissolved pattern can degrade Relationship (consensus trap), Self-Knowledge (pivot loop), or all Differentiation edges simultaneously (AI agreement). The failure mode identifies the missing vertex; the specific edge degradation identifies the mechanism.

5. **VCI deferral is itself a structural insight.** The decision to defer VCI isn't avoidance — it's the succession model applied to the repository's own development. Specifying a contribution index at pioneer stage would be premature formalization. The current tools (lineage, self-assessment, diagnostic questions) are the right tools for this succession stage. This is the framework being reflexive — applying its own developmental model to itself.
