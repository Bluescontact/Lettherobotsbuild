## THE CORE FRAMEWORK

Four vertices. Structural requirements — systems maintain these
or degrade predictably into specific failure modes.

| Vertex | Question | Failure Without It |
|--------|----------|--------------------|
| Differentiation | Does your position hold? | Dissolved |
| Connection | Is exchange genuine? | Isolated |
| Boundaries | Are limits visible? | Overflowing |
| Architecture | What persists without you? | Ephemeral |

Six edges — every vertex pair connected:
- Relationship (Differentiation ↔ Connection)
- Self-Knowledge (Differentiation ↔ Boundaries)
- Gift (Differentiation ↔ Architecture)
- Consent (Connection ↔ Boundaries)
- Circulation (Connection ↔ Architecture)
- Deployment (Boundaries ↔ Architecture)

**The somatic grounding:**
Each vertex has a nervous system correlate.
- Differentiation = regulated system that holds position without armoring
- Connection = ventral vagal, social engagement system online
- Boundaries = legible limits that signal safety, not threat
- Architecture = what remains when the regulated presence leaves

The framework doesn't just describe these capacities.
It is designed to *activate* them in the reader's body
before the reader has words for what's happening.

**The test:** Does engagement increase capacity to notice?
If capacity compounds → pattern operating.
If it depletes → something drifted.

### TASK 3: Somatic Architecture Integrity Check

This is the most important task and the easiest to skip.

The substrate being built is felt somatic recognition of safety.
Every page should either activate that recognition or support
the scaffolding that makes activation possible at scale.

For each node page that has content, check:
- Does it require body participation or only cognitive reading?
- Does it point at somatic knowing or try to contain it in text?
- Would a reader's nervous system register this as safe to open toward?

**Known somatic architecture already working:**
- Four diagnostic cards on index.html — body-first questions
- Breath gate on artifacts/practice.html — requires physical action
- Container transition on deep practice (dark contemplative space)
- Entry cards expand with practice instructions ("Notice what happens
  in your body when you say it")
- Forgotten Ground framing — presence as prior condition not achievement
- Examples page — story-first portraits that create recognition before
  framework language appears

**What to check on the node pages:**
The node pages (geometry, edges, failure-modes, etc.) were extracted
from the monolith as reference material. They may read as information
documents rather than somatic activation spaces. The content is correct.
The question is whether the reader's body is invited into the material
or only their mind.

**What you may do:**
If a node page lacks any body-directed question or somatic invitation,
you may add one — but only if you can do it without breaking the page's
structural voice. A single well-placed question ("Notice which of these
you recognize in your own body") does more than a paragraph of somatic
language. When in doubt, flag the gap in your session deposit rather
than adding language that doesn't land.

Minimum check per page:
Does it have at least one question directed at the reader's
body-level experience, not only their cognitive understanding?
